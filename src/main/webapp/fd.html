Frequently Asked Questions

What is a fixed deposit and why should I invest? ▼
A fixed deposit is a savings option where you deposit money for a fixed term and earn interest. It’s low risk and offers assured returns.
What is DICGC and which deposits are secured by them? ▼
DICGC insures bank deposits up to ₹5 lakh. It protects savings, current, recurring, and fixed deposits.
